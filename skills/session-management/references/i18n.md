# Session Management Translations

## Sessions Namespace

```yaml
# config/locales/sessions.en.yml
en:
  sessions:
    new:
      title: "Sign In"
      forgot_password: "Forgot your password?"
    form:
      email: "Email Address"
      password: "Password"
      sign_in: "Sign In"
    flash:
      create:
        success: "Signed in successfully."
        error: "Invalid email or password."
      destroy:
        success: "Signed out successfully."
      rate_limit: "Too many login attempts. Please try again in a few minutes."
```

## Settings Sessions Namespace

```yaml
# config/locales/settings.en.yml
en:
  settings:
    sessions:
      index:
        title: "Active Sessions"
        current_session: "Current session"
        this_device: "This device"
        sign_out: "Sign out"
        sign_out_all_others: "Sign out all other sessions"
        confirm_all: "Are you sure you want to sign out of all other devices?"
        last_active: "Active %{time} ago"
        created: "Created %{time} ago"
        inactive: "Inactive"
      flash:
        destroy:
          success: "Session terminated successfully."
          current: "You have been signed out."
        destroy_all:
          success: "All other sessions have been terminated."
```

      subject: "New login to your account"
